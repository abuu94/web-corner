Project Overview

EcoTrack is an innovative environmental monitoring system designed to track air quality, water purity, and biodiversity in real-time. Using IoT sensors and AI-powered analytics, we provide actionable insights for communities, researchers, and policymakers to make data-driven environmental decisions.

Our mission is to democratize environmental data and empower local communities to protect their natural resources. The system is currently being piloted in three regions across Tanzania.

Key Objectives:

  • Monitor air quality (PM2.5, CO₂, NO₂) in urban areas
  • Test water quality in rivers and lakes
  • Track biodiversity through acoustic monitoring

Key Features

Air Quality Monitoring

Real-time sensors measure particulate matter, gases, and pollutants with 99% accuracy.

Water Quality Testing

Continuous monitoring of pH, turbidity, dissolved oxygen, and heavy metals in water bodies.

Biodiversity Tracking

AI-powered acoustic sensors identify bird and animal species, tracking population changes.
